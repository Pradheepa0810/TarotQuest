# TarotQuest
Your Journey Through the Tarot Cards Begins Here.

Welcome, weary traveller
Come in from the road and rest awhile. 
The cottage keeps warm light in its windows, old stories in its rafters, and a patient hearth for every seeker who arrives with dust on their boots and questions in their heart.

Long ago, the cottage was said to stand where starlight touched the earth. There, the cards began to whisper: of courage, loss, hope, and hidden paths. 
Each reading is a lantern, and each lesson a step deeper into the lore of the arcana.

If you are ready, take up the deck and begin your tarot quest. The road ahead is mysterious, but the cottage will guide you toward wisdom one card at a time.
